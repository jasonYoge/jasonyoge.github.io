# Booksmarky

## Register

brand

## Target users

Chrome users who save many bookmarks and want AI-assisted folder suggestions without losing control over where links end up.

## Purpose

Marketing landing page for the Booksmarky Chrome extension — product introduction, Chrome Web Store install, credit pricing (Creem), and privacy policy.

## Brand personality

Calm, precise, trustworthy. Teal accent on clean stone surfaces — the extension UI, not generic SaaS cream.

## Anti-references

Purple-gradient AI slop, eyebrow labels on every section, identical feature-card grids, dark-mode-by-default tool aesthetic.

## Strategic design principles

- Match extension colors (teal accent, stone neutrals).
- Product screenshots carry the visual weight.
- Pricing and privacy must be explicit for Creem compliance.
